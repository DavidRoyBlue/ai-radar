sentient-agi/EvoSkill is a framework that automatically discovers and synthesizes reusable agent skills from *failed* trajectories. The mechanism — treat failures as a corpus, mine recurring failure modes, codify a corrective skill, evaluate whether the skill prevents recurrence — is one of the cleanest paths toward self-improving agents that doesn't require RL. Even if the reader doesn't adopt the framework, the failure-trajectory-to-skill pipeline is a pattern worth implementing manually as part of the radar's own feedback loop.
