Statewright (Show HN, 97 points) — visual state machines designed to make AI agents reliable. The author spent 20+ years on workflow systems before applying state-machine discipline to agentic problem-solving, where the brittleness of long autonomous runs has become the dominant failure mode. The pattern (explicit states + transitions + observability) is directly relevant for anyone building durable agent workflows; worth comparing to Temporal-style durable-execution and to LangGraph's state-graph approach before deciding which abstraction to adopt.
