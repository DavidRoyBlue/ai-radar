Trigger.dev now markets itself as building and deploying fully-managed AI agents and workflows. Durable execution under the hood (similar to Temporal) but with first-class agent primitives. Relevant to anyone who has felt the pain of running long autonomous agents on a laptop or under cron; the platform-vs-self-hosted tradeoff is real but the published primitives (durable retries, time-skipping in tests, status streaming) are a good reference design.
