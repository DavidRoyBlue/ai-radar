Simon Willison's piece on vibe coding and agentic engineering converging — argues the gap between disciplined agentic workflows (specs, tests, verification) and casual vibe-coding is narrowing in unhealthy ways. Useful framing for justifying TDD/verification skills to skeptics, and for spotting when a project's process is sliding from "agent doing engineering" to "agent generating slop". Concrete steal: the heuristics he proposes for telling the two apart could become checklist items in a verification skill.
