Curated dump of system prompts and model configurations from Claude Code, Cursor, Devin, Replit, Augment, Kiro, Lovable, and many others. Whether you're building a competing tool, designing CLAUDE.md / AGENTS.md files, or just want to understand how different agents are nudged differently, this is the single best primary source. Search for patterns in how the strongest harnesses encode discipline (verification, planning, tool selection) and lift the patterns into your own meta-prompts.
